Welcome to New Alley Group

Have Pride in Where We Live

We're bringing back the New Alley Group! Our mission is simple: keep our neighborhood clean, prevent illegal dumping, and create a beautiful place we can all be proud to call home.

How it works

It's really quite simple - just a few neighbors getting together to make a difference. We organize community clean-up activities and work together to keep our streets looking their best.

I'm 81 years young and have successfully run this group before - it really worked! I've got the experience and energy to coordinate everything, handle the paperwork, and yes, provide the tea, coffee, and biscuits for our meetings.

We need your help

What we're looking for now are enthusiastic younger neighbors to join us and help with the hands-on work. Whether you can spare an hour or a whole morning each month, every bit of help makes our community better.

Don't worry - I'll keep everyone organized and on track!
