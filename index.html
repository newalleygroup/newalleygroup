Have Pride in Where We Live

We're bringing back the New Alley Group! Our mission is simple: to keep our neighbourhood clean, prevent illegal dumping, and create a beautiful place we can all be proud to call home.

How it works

It's really quite simple -me Some neighbours getting together to make a difference .We organise local Street/Alleyways clean-ups, activities by working together our neighbourhood can look it's best

I'm 81 years young! and have successfully run this group before - it really worked! I've got the experience and energy to coordinate everything, handle the paperwork, and yes, provide the tea, coffee, and biscuits for our meetings.

We need your help

What we're looking for enthusiastic neighbours to join us and help with the hands-on work. Whether you can spare an hour or even more it all helps to make our local community cleaner.

Don't worry - I'll keep everyone organized and on track!
